Succeed in 3 Easy Steps

Gather Your Friends

Any Kamaiti works best when you have a group of friends and family, people you trust. The goal is simple: help each other out while helping yourself.

Pool Your Money

Get together each month and contribute to the pool. Give all this money to one person in the group.

Realize Your Dreams

Use this money to make your dreams come true. Need a vacation? Getting your car fixed? House needs repairs? Your friends just helped you out, and you have to pay zero interest. You just pay back every month.
